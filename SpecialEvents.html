Special Events

  • Wine and Cheese Social
    An evening of sampling fine wines and artisanal cheeses. Accompanied by a Professionalsommelier.
  • Cooking Class
    Our in-house chef provides a hands-on cooking class using seasonal, local ingredients. With a focus on baking, canning/preserving, and preparing a multi-course meal.

    local artists, craftspeople, farmers, are invited to set up booths and tables at the B&B so guests can shop for unique souvenirs and gifts while supporting the community.
  • Murder Mystery Dinner
    Guests dress up and take on characters for an interactive murder mystery event with a multi-course dinner. Costumes, props, and themed drinks set the mood.
